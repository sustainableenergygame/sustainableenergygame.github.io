How to play

The sustainable energy game is similar to rock paper scissors: you play the same, the rules are the same, but there's one major twist:

Instead of rock, paper and scissors, it's different sustainable energy sources. For example, rock is actually a Dam, paper is actually a Solar Panel and scissors is actually a Wind Turbine. Fascinating, am I right? Have fun!
