What is Climate Change?

Global climate change has observable effects on the environment. Glaciers have shrunk, ice on rivers and lakes is breaking up earlier, plant and animal ranges have shifted and trees are flowering sooner.

People around the world have been feeling the effects of climate change as well, lots of them have had to move and many people are having health risks due to air pollution which could be prevented with sustainable energy.

This is exactly why we want to make a contribution before its too late.
